About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Dragon Wok brings together the rich culinary traditions of India and China. Our journey began when our head chef, inspired by his travels across Asia, decided to create a unique fusion that celebrates the best of both worlds.

We take pride in using authentic spices and traditional cooking methods, while adding our own creative twist to each dish. Our commitment to quality ingredients and exceptional service has made us a favorite dining destination in the city.

Whether you're a fan of spicy Szechuan flavors or creamy Indian curries, our menu offers a delightful fusion that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more.
